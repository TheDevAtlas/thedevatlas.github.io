## How I Made AI Magic Cards

### Section 0: Project Goals

---

One of my favorite games is Magic: The Gathering. There are over 30,000 cards to build a deck and it creates one of the best enviroments to make friends and have fun. My idea for this project is to take some new AI tools and make some new AI generated Magic cards. This includes making sure that the AI makes game design descisions as well as artistic decisions.
