What is the Marching Cubes algorithm? It is an algorithm that breaks a function into chunks known as voxels. Then you can loop through the cubes and place one of these cases.

!["Marching Cubes Cases"](/photos/marchingcubes/2.png)

It was developed by William Lorensen and Harvey Cline at General Electric with the goal of replacing an algorithm called Cuberilles. It was licensed from the University of Pennsylvania and replacing it could save money and potentially make improvements

!["William Lorensen and Harvey Cline"](/photos/marchingcubes/3.png)

There is a whole story about how these two came up with the algorithm and if you want to know more, check out my [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cpu0KtIXcOw&t=384s) on it.
